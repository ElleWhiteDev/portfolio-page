Auto Cart

Web App

Auto-Cart is a smart household grocery management platform that simplifies meal planning and shopping for families and groups. Manage multiple households, plan weekly meals with chef assignments, and get your groceries with one click to your Kroger cart.

  • Multi-Household Management: Manage separate households (family, roommates, vacation homes) with independent recipes, lists, and meal plans.
  • AI-Powered Meal Planning: Plan weekly meals, assign cooking duties, and receive automated daily summaries and chef notifications.
  • Smart Recipe Sharing: Copy recipes between households and share via email with anyone, even non-users.
  • Kroger Cart Integration: Send your entire grocery list directly to your Kroger cart for pickup or delivery.
  • Intelligent Ingredient Consolidation: Automatically combines and organizes ingredients across multiple recipes.
  • Customizable Notifications: Control email preferences per household for meal plan updates and chef assignments.
  • Tech Stack: Frontend: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript | Backend: Python, Flask | Database: PostgreSQL | Hosting: Heroku | APIs: Kroger OAuth2, Product, Cart | AI: OpenAI GPT for recipe parsing and consolidation.
